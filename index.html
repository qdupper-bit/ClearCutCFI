Most candidates show up with a binder full of knowledge and no plan for delivering it. That's why the initial CFI practical test has one of the lowest first-attempt pass rates of any FAA checkride. Clear Cut CFI is built to fix exactly that.

FAQ

Common questions

When do the courses launch?

We're in the final build phase. Founders list members are notified first and get access before public release.

What is founder pricing?

Everyone who joins the email list before launch locks in 75% off every Clear Cut CFI course at launch. No code needed — the discount is tied to your email.

Is this for the initial CFI checkride?

Yes. Everything is built around the FAA Flight Instructor for Airplane Category ACS (FAA-S-ACS-25), with heavy emphasis on the Fundamentals of Instruction — the area that decides most initial CFI checkrides.

Are the free FOI quizzes really free?

Yes. One quiz for each FOI task, A through F, free with unlimited attempts. Each session pulls randomized questions from a larger bank, so repeats stay honest.

Can I use commercially produced study materials on my checkride?

The FAA's Aviation Instructor's Handbook states that commercially developed lesson plans are acceptable for most training situations, including flight instructor practical tests — as long as you understand and adapt the material. Our whole approach is making sure you can teach it, not just hold it.

Privacy

Privacy, plainly

What we collect. When you join the founders list, we collect your email address and the page you signed up from. That's it — the site has no accounts, no checkout, and no tracking cookies of its own.

How it's used. Your email is used to send launch news, founder pricing access, and occasional study content from Clear Cut CFI. We don't sell or share the list with anyone.

How it's stored. Form submissions are processed by our form provider and stored in our email tools. If you'd like your address removed, use the unsubscribe link in any email or contact us and we'll delete it.

Questions. Reach us any time via the contact page.
